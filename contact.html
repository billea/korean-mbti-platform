Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

How do I delete my account and data?

Email privacy@personatests.com with your account email address. We'll delete all your data within 30 days as required by GDPR and CCPA.

Are your personality tests accurate?

Our tests are based on established psychological frameworks but are for entertainment and self-reflection only. They're not professional psychological assessments.

Can I use test results for employment decisions?

No. Our tests are for entertainment only and should never be used for medical, employment, or legal decisions. Only licensed professionals can provide such assessments.

How do premium reports work?

Premium reports ($2.99) provide detailed insights and are delivered via email within 24 hours. All payments are securely processed through Stripe.
