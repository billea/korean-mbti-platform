Everything you need to know about PersonaTests

General Questions

What is PersonaTests?

PersonaTests is a digital platform offering scientifically-based personality assessments for entertainment and self-reflection. We provide tests like MBTI (16 Personalities), Big Five, Emotional Intelligence, and more to help you understand your unique traits and preferences.

Are your tests free?

Yes! All our basic personality tests are completely free to take. You'll get immediate results showing your personality type and basic insights. We also offer optional premium reports ($2.99) with detailed analysis, career suggestions, and relationship insights.

Do I need to create an account?

No account required! You can take any test anonymously and get your results immediately. Creating an account is optional and allows you to save your results, track your test history, and easily access premium reports.

Test Accuracy & Science

How accurate are your personality tests?

Our tests are based on established psychological frameworks with decades of research behind them. However, they're designed for entertainment and self-reflection, not professional assessment. Accuracy depends on honest self-reporting and can vary between individuals. Think of them as a fun starting point for self-discovery!

What's the difference between your MBTI test and the official one?

Our 16 Personalities test is inspired by Myers-Briggs theory but is not the official MBTI assessment. The official MBTI requires certification and costs $150+. Our version uses similar psychological concepts but is designed for general entertainment and self-exploration, not professional use.

Can I use these results for job applications or medical purposes?

Absolutely not. Our tests are for entertainment and personal insight only. They should never be used for employment decisions, medical diagnosis, legal matters, or any professional purposes. Only licensed professionals can provide assessments for such uses.

Privacy & Data Protection

What data do you collect?

For anonymous users: Only your test responses and results (stored locally in your browser). For registered users: Email address, name, age group, and test history. We never collect sensitive personal information beyond what's necessary for the service.

Do you sell my data?

Never. We don't sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your privacy is our priority. Revenue comes from optional premium reports and ethical advertising, not data sales.

How can I delete my data?

Contact us through our contact page with your account email. We'll permanently delete all your data within 30 days, as required by GDPR and CCPA. For anonymous users, simply clear your browser data.

Premium Reports

What's included in premium reports?

Premium reports ($2.99) include detailed personality analysis, career suggestions, relationship compatibility insights, personal growth tips, and communication style guidance. They're 8-12 pages of personalized content based on your results.

How do I get my premium report?

After completing a test, you'll see a premium report option. Payment is processed securely through Stripe. Your detailed report is delivered to your email within 24 hours. No subscription - it's a one-time purchase per test.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer full refunds within 30 days if you're not satisfied with your premium report. Contact us through our contact page with your order details. No questions asked!

Technical Support

The test won't load or is stuck. What do I do?

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. Our site works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). If problems persist, contact us through our contact page with your browser details.

Can I take tests on my phone?

Absolutely! Our site is fully mobile-optimized and works great on smartphones and tablets. The experience is identical to desktop - take tests anywhere, anytime.

I lost my test results. Can you recover them?

If you took the test with an account, we can help recover your results. Contact us through our contact page with your account email. For anonymous tests, results are only stored in your local browser and can't be recovered if lost.
